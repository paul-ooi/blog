Podcast bingeing

  • Smashing Magazine (web development)

  • Command Line Heroes (technology)

  • SEO Podcast (search engine optimization, web development)

  • Accessible.org Podcast (Accessibility)

  • 99% Invisible

  • A11y Rules Podcast (Accessibility)

  • Changelog - JS Party (web development)

  • devMode.fm (web development, freelancing)

  • Three Devs and a Maybe (web development, freelancing)

  • The Freelancers' Show (web development, freelancing)

  • Techmeme Ride Home (technology)

  • The Futur (design, freelancing)

Will update as my list grows...
